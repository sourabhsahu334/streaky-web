Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's exactly how Streaky handles your data.

Last updated: 12 July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Streaky – Daily Habit Tracker ("Streaky", "we", "us", or "the app"), operated by Neukaps, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign up, we collect your email address to create and secure your account.
  • Habit data: The habits, streaks, check-ins, and notes you create in the app are stored so we can sync them to your account.
  • Device & usage data: Basic technical data (such as device type and app version) may be collected to keep the app working reliably.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and sync your habits and streaks across sessions.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To improve the app's performance, stability, and features.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud backend provider. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Some data may also be cached locally on your device so the app works smoothly.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties except as required to operate the app (e.g. our cloud hosting provider) or to comply with the law.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

6. Children's Privacy

Streaky is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us
